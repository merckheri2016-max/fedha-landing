FAQ
Common questions
Do I need to download an app? +
No! Fedha works directly in your browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox on any phone or computer. You can also add it to your home screen to use it like an app.
Is my financial data safe? +
Yes. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. Only you can access your account with your PIN/password. We never share your information with anyone.
Can I use it on my phone and computer? +
Yes! Fedha works on any device with a browser. Your data syncs automatically so you can start on your phone and continue on your laptop.
How do I pay? +
We accept M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Visa and Mastercard debit/credit cards, and bank transfers. Payment is monthly and you can cancel anytime.
What if I want to cancel? +
You can cancel your subscription anytime from your profile settings. There are no contracts or hidden fees. You can also export all your data before cancelling.
Is there a free trial? +
Yes! Every new account gets a 7-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.
