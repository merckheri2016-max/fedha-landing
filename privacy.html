Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Your privacy matters to us. Fedha is committed to protecting your personal and financial data. We never sell your information to anyone.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password. When you use the app, we store the financial data you enter, including income records, expenses, budgets, savings goals, investments, and loan details.

We do not collect your bank account numbers, mobile money PINs, or any payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only to provide you with the Fedha service — to display your financial dashboard, generate reports, track your progress, and send you relevant notifications.

We never use your financial data for advertising, marketing to third parties, or any purpose other than providing you with the service.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase cloud servers with industry-standard encryption. All connections use HTTPS/SSL encryption.

Each user's data is protected by Row Level Security (RLS), which means no user can ever access another user's data. Your password is hashed and cannot be viewed by anyone, including us.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal or financial data with any third parties. Your data is yours alone.

5. Your Rights

Access your data — You can view all your data anytime through the app.

Export your data — You can export all your financial records from the Profile page.

Delete your account — You can request deletion of your account and all data by contacting us via WhatsApp.

Update your information — You can change your name, email, and password anytime from the Profile page.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Fedha uses minimal local storage only to keep you logged in and remember your language preference. We do not use tracking cookies or any third-party tracking tools.

7. Children's Privacy

Fedha is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Politique de Confidentialité

Dernière mise à jour : Avril 2026
